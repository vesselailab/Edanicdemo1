## n8n vs. AI Agent Action Engines: What's the Real Difference?

If your workflow is fully defined—trigger A leads to action B, then action C—tools like n8n and Make are hard to beat. They give you a visual canvas, drag-and-drop nodes, and precise control over data passing between steps. But the moment a task requires judgment, adaptation, or interaction with interfaces that don't have clean APIs, traditional workflow automation starts to struggle.

Manus approaches this differently. Instead of pre-wiring every node, it acts as an autonomous action engine: you describe what you want in natural language, and it figures out the steps, navigates interfaces, fills forms, clicks buttons, and adjusts when something unexpected happens.
