## When AI Avatars Might Not Be the Best Fit

While AI avatars are efficient for talking-head product explanations and marketing shorts, they are not a complete replacement for all types of product videos. If your products require physical demonstrations—such as showing the texture of a fabric, the assembly of a piece of furniture, or the unboxing experience—you will still need traditional live-action footage. In these cases, AI avatars work best as a supplementary tool to explain product details, benefits, and usage instructions, rather than showing the physical product in action. If you are considering live streaming instead of pre-recorded videos, you might want to look into digital human AI live streaming software recommendations.
