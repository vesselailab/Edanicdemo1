One workflow I'd never go back to doing manually is researching and cross-referencing 50+ companies to build a structured market report. Doing this by hand takes days, and standard AI loses context. With Manus, the Wide Research feature processes hundreds of sources in parallel and delivers a complete report in minutes.

## Why large-scale research is a manual nightmare
If you've ever tried to manually compile a competitive landscape report of 50 companies, you know the pain. You open dozens of tabs, copy-paste financial data, try to keep track of who does what, and then spend hours formatting. Even if you use a standard AI assistant, the context window limit causes quality to drop or hallucinations to appear. By the time you reach company 30, the AI has forgotten the details of company 1.

## How Wide Research changes the workflow
Manus solves this with its [Wide Research](/features/wide-research) capability. Instead of stuffing everything into one context window, it uses a parallel multi-agent architecture. This means it can handle hundreds of data sources at the same time. You just describe what you need, and it delivers a high-quality, non-degrading complete report and dataset within minutes. You get a structured output, not a pile of scattered notes. This is one workflow we see users rely on repeatedly in Industry Workflows & Solutions because it removes the research bottleneck entirely.

## Email-to-task: another automation you can't unsee
Another workflow that's hard to go back from is triaging emails. Usually, you read an email, decide if it needs research, and then create a task. With [Mail Manus](/features/mail), you simply forward or CC the email to a dedicated @manus.bot address. The AI automatically generates a summary, conducts research, and creates a structured task directly from your inbox. It turns a multi-step distraction into a single action. If you're curious about how people fit this into their daily routines, check out [How Are People Actually Using AI Agents](/use-cases/how-people-use-ai-agents-in-daily-workflows).

## When you might not need this
If you just need a quick summary of a single article or want to draft a simple email, a standard AI chat interface is fine. The value of these automations comes from scale and complexity. When a task requires cross-referencing data across multiple sources or continuously monitoring an inbox for action items, that's where an action engine like Manus shines. For simple, one-step queries, manual copy-pasting might still be faster.

## Frequently asked questions

**How many data sources can Wide Research handle?**

It is designed to process hundreds of data sources simultaneously, making it suitable for large-scale research tasks without the typical context degradation of standard AI models.

**Do I need to write code to use Mail Manus?**
